Famous Chicken Adobo Recipe

Description

A simple but delicious chicken adobo recipe made saucier than traditional adobo, enhancing its rich, umami-packed flavors for a more indulgent experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespooon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
